About EmotionRate

EmotionRate is an innovative project developed as part of the Affective Computing practica at LMU University. The application aims to provide insights into the emotions displayed by users while they watch a movie. Leveraging advanced artificial intelligence, EmotionRate interprets and analyzes user emotions in real-time.

Project Creators

How It Works

The EmotionRate app uses state-of-the-art AI technology to monitor users' facial expressions as they watch movies. The AI then processes this data to identify and interpret the emotions being displayed. This provides valuable feedback for understanding audience reactions and enhancing the movie-watching experience.
